Steel - Advanced Refractories

Indocast Risers Advanced Refractories business supplies the steel industry and other process industries with high performance refractory materials used for lining vessels such as blast furnaces, ladles and tundishes to enable them to withstand high temperatures and/or corrosive attack. These refractory lining materials are supplied in the form of powder mixes, which are spray-applied or cast onto the vessels to be lined (‘monolithics’), or in pre-cast shapes and bricks.

Steel - Flow Control

Our Flow Control products and systems are used extensively in the continuous casting process, enabling steel to be cast without interruption, while protecting it from the atmosphere when passing through the production process. Avoiding atmospheric contact significantly reduces contamination levels in the steel. Thus the quality, reliability and consistency of our products are critical in the quality of the finished metal being produced and the productivity, profitability and safety of our customers process .





Sleeve manufacturing

Indocast Risers specializes in precision manufacturing of refractory sleeves for foundry applications. Using advanced materials and design techniques, we ensure optimal metal flow and casting geometry. Recognized globally, our commitment to quality makes us a preferred supplier in the industry.

Steel - Digital Services

Our newly established Digital Services business complements existing product lines by bringing new services to our existing customers. Digital Services focuses on the capture of key manufacturing data, combining this with indocast’s strong presence and refractory expertise in metal casting to create new technologies and develop integrated expert process management systems.
