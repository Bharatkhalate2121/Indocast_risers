What We Do

Indocast Risers is a prominent name in the field of sleeve manufacturing for foundry applications in India. As a globally focused manufacturing entity, we specialize in the design and development of high-quality sleeves that play a crucial role in supporting and shaping castings. Recognized as a leading and preferred supplier, Indocast Risers has established a strong presence in India and other operational markets overseas. Our commitment to innovation and quality sets us apart in the industry, making us a trusted choice for sleeve solutions in the foundry sector.


We are committed to quality and focussed on producing highest quality products that add great value to our customers, anytime and every time. We have stringent quality control measures in place to ensure our Incoming raw materials and nished products goes through rigorous quality checks to ensure our customers receives consistent quality at all times.
